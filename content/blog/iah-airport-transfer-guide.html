Understanding IAH Airport Terminals

IAH has 5 passenger terminals (A, B, C, D, E), each serving different airlines:

  • Terminal A: Domestic carriers (American, Delta, Southwest)
  • Terminal B: Domestic carriers (Frontier, Spirit, United Express)
  • Terminal C: United Airlines hub
  • Terminal D: International carriers (Air France, Emirates, Lufthansa)
  • Terminal E: International carriers and United international

Your chauffeur will meet you at the designated pickup zone for your terminal, clearly marked and just minutes from baggage claim.
