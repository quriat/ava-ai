# AvaLimo Social Media Content Strategy

Platform Focus: Instagram + TikTok (Visual-first, luxury lifestyle)

---

## Brand Voice

- **Tone:** Luxury but approachable, confident, Houston-proud
- **Keywords in captions:** Arrive in style, Houston luxury, zero surge, 24/7, chauffeur (not "driver")
- **Emoji style:** Minimal, selective ✨🚗💨💎
- **CTA pattern:** "Book in 30 seconds" / "Link in bio" / "DM us"

---

| Pillar | % of Content | Example Topics |

## TikTok Video Ideas (15-60 seconds)

### Series: "POV: Your AvaLimo Ride"

**1. Airport Pickup POV**
- Hook: "POV: You just landed at IAH and your chauffeur is already here"
- Shots: Plane landing → text "Flight landed" → chauffeur holding name sign → walking to vehicle → opening door → settling in
- Music: Luxury ambient or trending TikTok sound
- CTA: "Book IAH pickup → link in bio"

**2. Wedding Arrival POV**
- Hook: "POV: You're arriving at your wedding in a Mercedes S-Class"
- Shots: Dress detail → vehicle pull-up → door opening → stepping out → crowd reaction
- Text overlay: "This could be you 👰"
- CTA: "Wedding packages starting at $450"

**3. Corporate Travel POV**
- Hook: "POV: Your client just landed and you're not scrambling to coordinate their pickup"
- Shots: Phone showing "Your chauffeur has arrived" → client getting in → smooth ride → office arrival
- Text: "Corporate accounts available"
- CTA: "DM 'CORPORATE' for account setup"

### Series: "AvaLimo vs. The Alternative"

**4. Surge Pricing Reality**
- Hook: "That $28 Uber to IAH became $94 because it rained"
- Split screen: Uber price surge → AvaLimo fixed price quote
- Text: "We don't surge. Ever."
- CTA: "Fixed rates → avalimo.net"

**5. The Driver Difference**
- Hook: "There's a difference between a driver and a chauffeur"
- Shots: Rideshare driver (casual, car messy) → AvaLimo chauffeur (suit, opening door, clean vehicle)
- Text overlays for each point
- CTA: "Experience the difference"

### Series: "Houston Hidden Gems"

**6. Date Night Route**
- Hook: "Best date night in Houston that doesn't involve you driving"
- Route: Downtown dinner → River Oaks theater → Hotel ZaZa → next morning brunch
- Shots: Night city lights from back seat, venues, couple happy
- CTA: "Book date night package"

**7. Bachelor Party Bus**
- Hook: "This is how Houston does bachelor parties"
- Shots: Group getting in Sprinter → BYOB setup → music → montage of Houston nightlife spots
- Text: "14 friends. 1 Sprinter. 0 DUIs."
- CTA: "Party bus from $195/hr"

**8. Wine Tour Day Trip**
- Hook: "Texas Hill Country wine tour, chauffeur-driven"
- Shots: Houston morning departure → scenic drive → wineries → tastings → sunset return
- Text: "Sip safely. We'll drive."
- CTA: "Book wine tour → link in bio"

### Series: "Vehicle Showcases"

**9. Mercedes S-Class Tour**
- Hook: "Let me show you why this is the executive choice"
- Shots: Walkaround exterior → interior details (leather, ambient lighting) → rear legroom → trunk space
- Voiceover or text pointing out features
- CTA: "S-Class from $100"

**10. Escalade vs. Suburban**
- Hook: "Why our Escalade isn't just any SUV"
- Comparison shots: Standard SUV vs. Escalade (cleanliness, space, features)
- Text: "6 passengers. Infinite legroom."
- CTA: "SUV from $115"

**11. Sprinter Party Mode**
- Hook: "This van fits your entire friend group"
- Shots: Empty Sprinter → transformation (LED lights, speakers, coolers) → group having fun
- Text: "14 seats. 1 driver. 0 worries."
- CTA: "Sprinter from $195"

## Instagram Content Ideas

### Feed Posts (High-quality photography)

**1. Vehicle Spotlight Carousel**
- Slide 1: Hero shot of vehicle at Houston landmark (e.g., S-Class at Hermann Park)
- Slide 2: Interior detail shot
- Slide 3: Feature list (legroom, amenities)
- Slide 4: Pricing + CTA
- Caption template: "The [Vehicle] isn't just transportation — it's an experience. Perfect for [use case]. Starting at $[price]. Book link in bio."

**2. Client Testimonial Graphics**
- Design: Clean, luxury aesthetic with gold accents
- Quote: "Service was impeccable..." — James R.
- Rating: 5 stars
- Service type: Airport Transfer
- CTA: "Experience it yourself → link in bio"

**3. "This or That" Engagement Posts**
- Image: Split screen S-Class vs. Escalade
- Caption: "Business meeting: S-Class or Escalade? Comment 👇"
- Engagement strategy: Reply to every comment

**4. Houston Event Calendar**
- Monthly graphic: "What's happening in Houston this month"
- List events (Astros games, concerts, galas)
- Add: "Need a ride? We got you."
- CTA: "Event packages available"

**5. Before/After (Vehicle Prep)**
- Photo 1: Vehicle entering detailing bay
- Photo 2: Same vehicle, spotless, ready for client
- Caption: "Every ride is immaculate. No exceptions."

### Reels (Instagram's TikTok competitor)

Repurpose all TikTok content as Reels. Add:
- Houston-specific hashtags
- Trending audio (if applicable)
- Cover images with text overlay

### Stories (Daily/Weekly)

**Daily Story Ideas:**
- Morning: "Good morning Houston, who's riding with us today?"
- Midday: Behind-the-scenes (vehicle prep, chauffeur briefing)
- Evening: "Another day of perfect arrivals ✨"

**Weekly Story Series:**
- **Monday:** "This week in Houston" (event preview)
- **Wednesday:** "Vehicle Wednesday" (spotlight one vehicle)
- **Friday:** "Weekend plans?" (date night, event packages)
- **Saturday:** "Wedding weekend" (if applicable)

**Interactive Story Features:**
- Polls: "S-Class or Escalade?"
- Quizzes: "How much do you think IAH → Downtown costs?"
- Q&A: "Ask us anything about luxury transportation"
- Countdown: "Wedding season starts in [X] days"

## User-Generated Content (UGC) Strategy

**How to get clients to post:**
1. Include branded card in vehicle: "Loved your ride? Tag @avalimo for a feature + 10% off your next booking"
2. After wedding/event: Text bride/groom "Would love to feature your arrival photos with credit! Reply with your photographer's info"
3. Create a branded hashtag: `#AvaLimoMoments`
4. Repost UGC to your stories with permission
