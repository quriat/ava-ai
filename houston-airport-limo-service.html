Frequently Asked Questions

How early should I book my airport limo?

We recommend booking at least 24 hours in advance. However, we do accommodate last-minute requests when possible — call (832) 567-8050 for same-day availability.

What if my flight is delayed?

No problem! We track all flights in real-time. Your chauffeur adjusts pickup time accordingly at no extra charge.

Where will my chauffeur meet me at IAH?

For domestic flights: We meet you at baggage claim. For international flights: We meet you at customs exit. Your chauffeur will have a sign with your name.

Do you charge extra for luggage?

No. Standard luggage (2 checked bags + 1 carry-on per passenger) is included. Please let us know if you have oversized items like golf clubs or skis.

Can I book a round-trip airport transfer?

Absolutely! Round-trip bookings get preferred scheduling and simplified checkout.

Do you serve both IAH and Hobby Airport?

Yes — we provide premium limo service to both George Bush Intercontinental (IAH) and William P. Hobby Airport (HOU).
