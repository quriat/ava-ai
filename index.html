{% elif content_key == 'faq' %}

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I book a ride?▼
You can book online using our booking form, call us at (832) 567-8050, or email adam@avalimo.net. Online booking is fastest — just fill in your details and we'll confirm within minutes.
Do you service both IAH and Hobby?▼
Yes! We cover both George Bush Intercontinental (IAH) and William P. Hobby (HOU) airports. We also serve Sugar Land, The Woodlands, Katy, Missouri City, Pearland, Galveston, League City, Baytown, Spring and Cypress.
Do you track flights for airport pickups?▼
Absolutely. We monitor your flight in real-time so we're always there when you land — even if your flight is early or delayed. No extra charge.
How much does it cost?▼
Pricing depends on vehicle type, distance, and duration. We offer transparent flat-rate pricing with zero surge fees. Contact us for a quote — we typically respond within minutes.
Can I cancel or modify my booking?▼
Yes. You can cancel or modify your booking up to 24 hours before the scheduled pickup time for a full refund. Late cancellations may incur a fee.
Are your drivers licensed and insured?▼
Every chauffeur is fully licensed, insured and professionally trained, with thorough background checks and regular vehicle inspections.
{% elif content_key == 'policy' %}

Company Policy

Booking & Reservations

All reservations are confirmed upon receipt of a booking deposit. Deposits are fully refundable with 24-hour cancellation notice. Same-day bookings are subject to availability.

Cancellation Policy

Cancel or modify up to 24 hours before pickup for a full refund. Cancellations within 24 hours may incur a charge of 50% of the fare. No-shows are charged in full.

Waiting Time

Airport pickups include 60 minutes of complimentary waiting time from landing. All other pickups include 15 minutes. Additional waiting time is billed at $1 per minute.

Smoking & Cleanliness

All vehicles are strictly non-smoking. A $250 cleaning fee will be charged for smoking, spills, or excessive mess.

Privacy

We respect your privacy. Personal information collected during booking is used solely for providing our services and will never be shared with third parties without your consent.
